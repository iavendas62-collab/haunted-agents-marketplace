## ðŸ’¡ Quick Tips

### Recording
- **Speak clearly** at moderate pace
- **Move cursor slowly** and deliberately
- **Pause briefly** on important information
- **Practice first** - do 2-3 dry runs

### Editing
- **Cut aggressively** - remove all dead time
- **Keep it simple** - no fancy effects
- **Add text overlays** - intro, app labels, closing
- **Check duration** - must be under 3:00

### Upload
- **Use template** - description template in YouTube guide
- **Include all links** - GitHub, demos, CLI, docs
- **Make it PUBLIC** - judges need to view it
- **Verify everything** - watch full video after upload
